How I work

My design process

A structured, iterative approach that puts the user at the center of every decision.

01

Discover

User research, competitor analysis, and stakeholder interviews to understand the problem deeply.

02

Define

Synthesize insights into personas, user journeys, and a clear design brief with goals and constraints.

03

Ideate

Sketch wireframes, explore multiple concepts, and choose the strongest direction through critique.

04

Design

Build high-fidelity screens with a consistent design system, motion, and micro-interactions.

05

Test & ship

Usability testing, iterate on feedback, and hand off to developers with complete specs.
